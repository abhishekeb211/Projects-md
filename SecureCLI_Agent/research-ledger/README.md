# Research Ledger Directory

This directory contains the AegisCLI Research Ledger and related documentation.

## Files

- `ledger-v0.md` - Initial Research Ledger (Week 0)
- `ledger-v1.md` - Research Ledger after Phase 1 (Week 4)
- `ledger-v2.md` - Research Ledger after Phase 2 SLR (Week 12)
- `constraints-mapping.md` - Research Scope & Constraint Mapping (Week 0)

## Usage

The Research Ledger is a **living document** that should be updated as:
- New decisions are made
- Validations are completed
- Assumptions are confirmed or refuted
- Dependencies are updated

## Version History

- **v0** (Week 0): Initial setup with definitions, baselines, assumptions, decisions
- **v1** (Week 4): Updated after Phase 1 (locked decisions, RQs, contributions)
- **v2** (Week 12): Updated after Phase 2 SLR (key findings, gap confirmation)
